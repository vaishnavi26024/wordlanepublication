What is Word Lane Publication?
Word Lane Publication is a publishing platform for authors from around the world. We make it easy for you to create, publish and distribute your book.
Who owns the rights to my book?
You retain all the rights to your book. We help distribute your book through our partners.
What is an ISBN and will my book get an ISBN number?
ISBN stands for International Standard Book Number. It is a 13-digit number used to identify books.
Can I track how many books I've sold?
Yes. You can track your book sales and earnings from the author dashboard.
How do I set the selling price of my book?
You can decide the selling price based on production cost and your expected profit.
How many copies of my book will you print?
Books are printed using Print-on-Demand technology to ensure availability without large inventory.
Will I receive post-publishing support?
Yes. We provide continuous support to authors even after the book is published.
